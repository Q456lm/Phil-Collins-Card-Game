Each player starts at with 7 cards and the players goal is to use those cards as well as any additonal cards they must draw to release albums. 
<br></br>
Each player has 8 albums they must released. Once all of a players albums are released they win the game.
<br></br>
Heres the twist, as you release albums you lose empathy, and having less empathy means you can have less cards in your hands. Your empathy starts at 22 but will go down quickly.
<br></br>
To Play run game.java
<br></br>
The Albums:
<br></br>
1 - Face Value - Use a divorce triggering card and wait two turns. //Fully Implemented
2 - Hello, I Must be Going! - If your empathy >= 5 then deposit at least 5 cards in one turn, otherwise deposit all cards for your empathy in one turn i.e. 3 empathy = 3 cards deposited. Other people's turns count. //Partily Implemented
3 - No Jacket Required - Run Out Of Cards //Fully Implemented
4 - …But Seriously - Don’t use any cards for 2 turns //Fully Implemented
5 - Both Sides - If all the players have the players have 4 or less cards you can make this album //Fully Implemented
6 - Dance into the Light - Don’t Draw Cards for 5 turns //Fully Implemented
7 - Testify - Have no players affect you for two turns. //Fully Implemented
8 - Going Back - Gain 5 cards in one turn, even if you have to deposit the cards. //Could be either lowkey
<br></br>
The Cards:
<br></br>
1 - In The Air Tonight - Draw 2 song cards and start a divorce
2 - I Don’t Care Anymore - Release an album
3 - Take Me Home - All players deposit all cards and draw 5 new cards
4 - Another Day In Paradise - All Players except for you deposit one card.
5 - One More Night - If you have 2 cards or less (after using this card), draw 6 cards of any type.
6 - Easy Lover - Target One Player to Deposit Three Cards
7 - Against All Odds - Stop the effects against you for all other cards on this turn.
8 - Sussudio - Draw 3 cards of any type (Maybe only if they can spell it)
9 - Don’t Lose My Number - However many cards are in your hand when you use this card if how many cards must be in your hand after 2 turns.
10 - I Wish It Would Rain Down - Pick a Player and Steal one of their album cards
11 - Do You Remember? - Look at another player's cards
12 -  Two Hearts - If Players > 2: Pick 2 Players and Steal 1 song Card from each of them. If only 2 players: Steal 2 song cards from the other player
13 - You Can’t Hurry Love - You can use 2 cards this turn after this.
14 - That’s All - Deposit 2 song cards and draw 2 new song cards and start a divorce
15 - I Can’t Dance - Pick another player to deposit 3 song cards
16 - Invisible Touch - Stops Divorces
